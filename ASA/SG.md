## Sovereignty Gate (SG)

The Sovereignty Gate (SG) is the decision interface between drift detection and human oversight.

Its purpose is to ensure that final authority in human–AI interaction remains external to the AI system.

When the Drift Observability Layer (DOL) detects potential instability, the SG layer evaluates the signal and performs one or more actions:

• Alerting the human operator  
• Flagging the interaction state as unstable  
• Allowing external supervision or intervention  
• Preserving the human decision authority

The SG does not control the AI model internally. Instead, it acts as an external safety interface that exposes system state to the human participant.

Design principle:

Human agency remains structurally independent from the AI system.
