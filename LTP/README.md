# LTP — Latent Threshold Protocol

LTP is a passive observational protocol designed to detect early stages of relational drift in long-horizon human–AI interactions.

Instead of evaluating single responses, LTP observes trajectory changes across dialogue cycles.

The protocol focuses on identifying signals such as:

- intent mutation
- semantic drift
- compression of decision cycles
- loss of epistemic anchors

LTP does not intervene in model behavior.

Its purpose is to expose drift trajectories before they become operational failures.

Within the HumansAI framework, LTP acts as the first layer of relational stability monitoring.
